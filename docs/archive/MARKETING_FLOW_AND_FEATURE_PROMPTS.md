# HexaBill Marketing: Super Flow, Screenshots, Video & Feature Presentation Prompts

Use this doc for the full marketing flow, capture steps, and **step-by-step feature presentation prompts** (for copy, slides, or AI-assisted page generation).

---

## 1. Overall page flow (super flow)

```
Hero (headline + CTA)
    ↓
Problem / Solution (one line)
    ↓
Product demo video (60–90 s) — optional in hero or features
    ↓
Features (each = headline + screenshot + step-by-step bullets + CTA)
    ↓
Pricing / Trial CTA
    ↓
Demo request form or Contact
    ↓
Footer
```

## 2. Screenshot & video flow (what to capture)

### Screenshot order (use same demo tenant for all)

| Step | Screen | File name | Notes |
|------|--------|-----------|--------|
| 1 | Login | (optional) `login-desktop.png` | Clean login; no sensitive data |
| 2 | Dashboard | `dashboard-desktop.png` | Period: Today or This week; cards with numbers |
| 3 | Dashboard (mobile) | `dashboard-mobile.png` | DevTools or real device |
| 4 | Branches & Routes | `branches-routes-desktop.png` | At least one branch, one route |
| 5 | Products list | `products-desktop.png` | Few products visible |
| 6 | POS | `pos-desktop.png` | Cart with 1–2 items; customer selected |
| 7 | POS (mobile) | `pos-mobile.png` | Same as above on mobile |
| 8 | Sales Ledger / Invoices | `invoices-desktop.png` | List of invoices |
| 9 | Reports (e.g. Summary or Sales) | `reports-desktop.png` | Filters collapsed or expanded; chart/table |
| 10 | Customer Ledger | `ledger-desktop.png` | One customer selected; ledger visible |

### Video flow (60–90 seconds)

1. **0:00–0:10** — Login (blur or skip password).
2. **0:10–0:25** — Dashboard: show cards, period selector, “Get started” or metrics.
3. **0:25–0:35** — Quick open Branches & Routes (one branch, one route).
4. **0:35–0:50** — Products → open POS → add product → select customer → Create Invoice.
5. **0:50–1:00** — Open Sales Ledger or Reports → show one report (e.g. Sales or Summary).
6. **1:00–1:30** — Optional: Customer Ledger, then end on Dashboard or CTA slide.

**Tool:** OBS, Loom, or similar. Export as `hexabill-demo.mp4`; thumbnail `hexabill-demo-thumb.png`.

## 3. Step-by-step feature presentation prompts

Each block below is a **prompt** for one feature: use as-is for copy, or feed to an AI to generate a short section (headline + bullets + CTA).

---

### Feature 1: Dashboard

**Headline:** See your business at a glance.

**Screenshot:** `dashboard-desktop.png` (optional: `dashboard-mobile.png`).

**Step-by-step (for presentation/copy):**
1. Log in and land on the Dashboard.
2. View today’s (or this week’s) sales, returns, expenses, purchases, and profit in one place.
3. Use the period selector: Today, This week, This month, or Custom.
4. Use the “Get started” checklist until setup is complete.
5. Jump to POS, Reports, or other areas from the same screen.

**One-liner:** *See sales, returns, expenses, and profit at a glance. Filter by today, week, or month.*

**CTA:** Start with a free trial.

---

### Feature 2: VAT-compliant invoicing

**Headline:** VAT-compliant invoices, ready for the Gulf.

**Screenshot:** `invoices-desktop.png`.

**Step-by-step:**
1. Create invoices from POS or Sales Ledger.
2. Company name, address, and TRN (VAT number) appear on every invoice.
3. UAE 5% VAT is calculated automatically; multi-currency supported (e.g. AED).
4. Print or download as PDF for your records and for the customer.
5. Track status: Paid, Pending, Partial.

**One-liner:** *Create and print VAT invoices with TRN. UAE 5% and multi-currency supported.*

**CTA:** Book a demo.

---

### Feature 3: Branches & routes

**Headline:** Branches and routes in one app.

**Screenshot:** `branches-routes-desktop.png`.

**Step-by-step:**
1. Add your first branch (e.g. warehouse or office).
2. Add routes under a branch for delivery or field sales.
3. Assign staff and customers to routes.
4. Use reports filtered by branch or route.
5. Scale from one location to many without changing tools.

**One-liner:** *Manage branches and routes. Assign staff and customers to routes for delivery and field sales.*

**CTA:** See how it works.

---

### Feature 4: POS billing

**Headline:** Fast POS billing for counter and field sales.

**Screenshot:** `pos-desktop.png`, `pos-mobile.png`.

**Step-by-step:**
1. Open POS from the sidebar or dashboard.
2. Search for products and add them to the cart.
3. Select or add a customer.
4. Apply discounts or notes if needed.
5. Click “Create Invoice” — invoice is generated and printed or saved as PDF.

**One-liner:** *Quick POS billing with product search, customer selection, and payment modes.*

**CTA:** Try POS free.

---

### Feature 5: Reports & analytics

**Headline:** Reports that drive decisions.

**Screenshot:** `reports-desktop.png`.

**Step-by-step:**
1. Open Reports from the sidebar.
2. Choose a date range and optional filters (branch, route, product, customer).
3. Switch tabs: Summary, Sales, Product, Customers, Expenses, Branch, Route, Aging, P&amp;L, Outstanding, Returns, Damage.
4. Export to Excel or PDF where available.
5. Use data for cash flow, aging, and route performance.

**One-liner:** *Sales report, profit & loss, outstanding bills, and staff performance by route.*

**CTA:** Explore Reports.

---

### Feature 6: Customer ledger

**Headline:** One place for customer balance and history.

**Screenshot:** `ledger-desktop.png`.

**Step-by-step:**
1. Open Customer Ledger from the sidebar.
2. Search or select a customer.
3. View ledger entries, invoices, and payments in one place.
4. Record payments and see balance update.
5. Use for collections, statements, and aging.

**One-liner:** *Customer statements, payment history, and aging.*

**CTA:** Simplify collections.
